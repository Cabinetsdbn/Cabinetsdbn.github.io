ABOUT US

Eco Production

What does this meen? So our aim is to produce and manufacture cabinets and furniture as ecologically as possible. Using the latest technology, we achieve this goal and reduce the wastage of boards and materials. In return we do our part to save the enviroment, and your pocket, without compremising quality. Recycling is a big part of our business!

Bespoke Design

Because no two people are the same, and no two kitchen or bedroom cabinets layouts are the same, we have to be able to flex and mould into your unique and custom requirements. Our products ranges from simple and affordable to outlandish and exquisit. We offer Melamine, PVC wrap, Duco painted, Acrylic coated and Solid Wood cabnets. We also cater for all your counter top requirements!

One Stop Shop

A new kitchen usually goes hand in hand with a minor revamp. Weather it is to change out the old warn out tiles or sink, or a complete re-design, we will have the solution for you. Our skilled teams has years of experience in this field, and we will gladly project manage your whole revamp for you. We make use of excilent, trustworthy artisans to complete your electrical and plumbing needs too.
